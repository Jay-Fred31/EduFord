Our Courses

Eduford offers a wide array of courses across various disciplines, ensuring that you can find a program that aligns with your interests and career goals.

Undergraduate

Undergraduate courses at Oxford typically follow a three-year structure for most subjects. Some courses, such as Medicine, may have a longer duration.

Degree

Eduford offers graduate courses in a wide range of disciplines, spanning humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, engineering, and more.

Post Graduate

Eduford offers a variety of graduate-level courses, including master's degrees (MSc, MPhil, etc.), doctoral degrees (PhD, DPhil), and professional degrees (such as MBA, Law, Medicine, etc.).

Our Culture Rich Facilities

Eduford is rich in history and boasts iconic landmarks such as the Radcliffe Camera, the Sheldonian Theatre, and the University Church of St Mary the Oceans

State of the art library

The Boldeens Library, one of the oldest and most renowned libraries in the world, serves as the main research library for the university.

Clean and Serene Cafeteria

Our cafeteria provides a range of options, from quick bites to full meals, and they are often popular spots for students to gather, socialize, and study.

State of the Sport Facilities

Eduford offers a variety of sports facilities, including gyms, sports halls, swimming pools, outdoor sports fields, and more.
